How Color Affects Mood and Behavior

Color psychology studies how different colors can evoke various emotions and influence behavior. For example, red is often associated with passion and energy, while blue evokes feelings of calmness and trust. Understanding these effects is crucial in many fields, such as design, marketing, and therapy.

Common Color Associations

  • Red: Passion, excitement, love
  • Blue: Calmness, trust, stability
  • Green: Nature, growth, freshness
  • Yellow: Happiness, optimism, caution
